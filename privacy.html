Privacy Policy

Lyrics Video Tool

Last updated: March 2026

What we collect

  • TikTok authentication token (stored locally on your device only)

What we do NOT collect

  • Personal information
  • Usage data or analytics
  • Video content or viewing history

Your authentication token is stored only on your local device and is used solely to upload videos to your TikTok account. No data is transmitted to any server other than TikTok's official API.

Data Retention

Your authentication data is stored locally and can be deleted at any time by removing the token file from your device.

Third Party Services

This tool interacts only with TikTok's official Content Posting API. No other third-party services receive your data.
